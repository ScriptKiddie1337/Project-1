Regents Pizzeria

Forrest Smith

Oh so good. Great Chicago Style pies. Pizza Pies... My daughter has to eat gluten free and she said their Gluten Free options are great too. I've tasted them and have to agree (for Gluten Free). Staff has always been fast and friendly. I don't drink, but have heard their selection is good too.

Isaac DeLaCruz

If you are a Pizza Lover, do yourself a favor and try this place. Definitely in my top 5 pizza spots. They do Chicago style right. I had a meat lovers and Popeye's and they both tasted heavenly, cooked to perfection. What better way to treat yourself after a beautiful hike in Torrey Pines?
